About This Project

The Fuel Consumption Prediction System is a machine learning-powered web platform that estimates vehicle fuel usage based on technical specifications. It helps users make cost-effective and eco-conscious decisions using real-world vehicle data.

üîç Dataset Overview

  • 1. Dataset: Contains 2022 Fuel Consumption Ratings information.
  • 2. Model Year: Represents the production year of a specific vehicle model.
  • 3. Make: The brand or manufacturer of the vehicle.
  • 4. Model: A specific version of a vehicle produced under a given make.
  • 5. Vehicle Class: Categorizes vehicles by size, body style, and intended use.
  • 6. Engine Size: Total volume of air and fuel drawn into the engine cylinders (in L or cc).
  • 7. Cylinders: Engine chambers where combustion takes place.
  • 8. Transmission: System that transmits engine power to wheels for movement.
  • 9. Fuel Type: Type of fuel used: Z = Premium Gasoline | D = Diesel | E = Ethanol | X = Regular Gasoline
  • 10. Fuel Consumption (City): Fuel usage in city driving (L/100 km).
  • 11. Fuel Consumption (Hwy): Fuel usage on highways (L/100 km).
  • 12. Fuel Consumption (Comb - L/100 km): Combined fuel usage in L/100 km.
  • 13. Fuel Consumption (Comb - mpg): Combined usage in miles per imperial gallon.
